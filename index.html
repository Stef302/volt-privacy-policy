Introduction

Welcome to Volt, a simple note-taking app designed with your privacy in mind. We are committed to protecting your privacy and want to be transparent about how we handle (or rather, do not handle) your data. This Privacy Policy explains that all your notes and data are stored locally on your device and are not collected or transmitted by us.

Data Collection and Usage

Volt **does not collect, store, or transmit any of your personal information or the content of your notes** to any external servers or third parties. Everything you create in Volt stays on your device, under your control.

The app allows you to export your notes as `.volt` files. This action is initiated by you, and the files are saved to your device's local storage as per your instruction. We do not have access to these files unless you choose to share them externally yourself.

Data Security

Since all data is stored locally on your device, the security of your data largely depends on the security of your device itself. We recommend you use your device's built-in security features (like passcodes and biometric authentication) to protect your information. The `.volt` file format is designed to be a secure container for your notes when you export them.

Children's Privacy

Volt is not directed at individuals under the age of 13 (or the relevant age in your jurisdiction). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you are a parent or guardian and you are aware that your child has provided us with personal information (which should not be possible through the app's design), please contact us so that we can take necessary actions.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes. Changes to this Privacy Policy are effective when they are posted on this page.
